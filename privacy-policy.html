Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 December 2025

CEYE DIGICROP ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles user data.

Information We Collect

Camera Access

Our App requests access to your device’s camera to allow users to capture images required for app functionality, such as image-based analysis and related features.

  • Captured images are used only for user-initiated actions.
  • We do not access the camera without your explicit permission.
  • Images are not shared with third parties without user consent.

Location Access

Our App may request access to your device’s location to provide location-based functionality, such as contextual insights or region-specific analysis.

  • Location data is collected only when required for app features.
  • We do not track your location continuously.
  • Location data is not sold or shared with third parties.

How We Use Information

The information collected through camera and location permissions is used solely to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Enable features explicitly requested by the user

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Any data processed is handled securely and only for the duration necessary to fulfill its intended purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Data is shared only if required by law or with your explicit consent.

User Control

You can control or revoke camera and location permissions at any time through your device settings. Denying permissions may limit certain app features.

Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
